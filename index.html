Our Donate Plan

Our plan is to make each lives stable with every essential needs. We have 3 causes:

Food for a family

Distributing food to feed every people in a way so that they can survive. We will provide foods like meat, bread, rice and dairy products on everyday basis. We also have food rescue organizations who provide food rescue programming, which includes retrieving excess food from some grocery shops and delivering donated food to the needy.

Shelter for a family

Sylhet is now having a disaster and it is not even capable of living. As this natural calamity ruined everything, we will help re-locate each family in different area. Now, we are creating a building with minimum shelter for 1200 people at the outskirt of sylhet, so that they can stay for the time being the calamity ends. Re-locating them is what needed now.

Education for a child

Children are suffering from study-break for this crisis. We have managed a contact with a school to let the children's continue their studies, so the loss time gets covered and they dont suffer from a study break. Our education team will provide every necessary needs for each children’s education cause and help the children grow without having the gap.
